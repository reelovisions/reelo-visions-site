SMS Terms & Consent

Program: Reelo Visions Alerts

Purpose: We send appointment confirmations, reminders, and day-of “technician on the way” notifications for service visits.

How to Opt In

By checking the consent box on our contact/booking form and submitting your phone number, you agree to receive SMS messages from Reelo Visions at the number provided.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by appointment activity (typically 1-6 msgs per appointment).

Charges

Message and data rates may apply.

Help / Stop

Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to cancel. After you send STOP, you will receive one final message confirming your opt-out.

Support

Questions? Email hello@reelovisions.com or call (612) 840-6268.

Privacy

We use your phone number only to deliver the messages described above and to support your appointment. We do not sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Eligibility

You must be the account holder or have the account holder’s permission to enroll.

Carriers

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
