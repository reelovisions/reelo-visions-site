Verbal SMS Consent — Script

Brand: Reelo Visions LLC · HVAC receptionist

Exact script (read verbatim on every intake call)

“Before I schedule your appointment, may we send you text messages about your appointment and service updates from {HVAC Company Name} at this number {repeat caller’s number}?
Messages may include confirmations, reminders, arrival updates, and follow-ups. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.
You can reply STOP to opt out at any time, and HELP for help.
We’ll never share or sell your number.
Do I have your permission to send texts to this number?”

If caller says “Yes” (required follow-up)

“Thank you. I’m noting your verbal consent. You’ll get a confirmation text in a moment. Please reply YES to confirm. You can reply STOP any time to opt out.”

Double opt-in (first confirmation SMS)

{HVAC Company Name}: You agreed to receive SMS for scheduling & updates at {phone}.
Reply YES to confirm. Msg&Data rates may apply. Freq varies. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.
Terms: https://reelovisions.com/sms-terms  Privacy: https://reelovisions.com/privacy

How consent is stored

We log for each consent: phone (E.164), call SID or recording URL, timestamps, agent/AI ID, script version, yes/no result, and the double opt-in result (“YES”) with timestamp. Data retained up to 10 years. STOP/HELP supported on every message; opt-outs are honored immediately and also saved in our suppression list. START/UNSTOP are honored per Twilio compliance.
